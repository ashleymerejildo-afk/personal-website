About Me

From bedside to keyboard, the standard doesn't change.

I've spent my career making sure people are taken care of — now I'm learning to build the systems that do it at scale.

As a CNA, Phlebotomy Technician, and EKG Technician, I learned to work under pressure, read a room, and get details right the first time — because in healthcare, there is no version two. That standard follows me into every line of code I write.

I'm currently sharpening that instinct at Pursuit, an intensive software engineering fellowship, where I'm learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, APIs, and AI tools. I learn quickly, stay organized under deadline, and communicate clearly with technical and non-technical people alike — skills built over years of patient care and customer service, now aimed at software.
